Rules:
The game starts with the computer selecting a random word. The word is displayed as a series of underscores, each representing a letter. Your task is to guess the word by suggesting one letter at a time. If your guess is correct, the computer fills in the appropriate underscores with the letter. If your guess is incorrect, the computer adds a part to the "hangman" figure. The game continues until you've either guessed the entire word or the hangman is fully drawn. You win if you guess the word before the hangman is completed. If the hangman is finished before you guess the word, you lose. The user also has the option to receive a hint, which is the definition of the word. Have fun playing!
